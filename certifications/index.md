# Certifications

This page is the public index of the Declarations of Conformity (DoCs) and their supporting EMC assessments for the product family listed below. Each entry links to the signed declaration issued for the European Union (CE marking under the applicable EU directives) and, where available, for the United Kingdom (UKCA marking under the corresponding UK statutory instruments). The EMC assessment document that accompanies each DoC records the product's design characteristics, emission and immunity reasoning, and the harmonised or designated standards applied.

Products are organised by family and hardware revision. When shown, the SKU line identifies the specific unit referenced on the corresponding declaration for traceability.
